PHK Bank · PWB Relationship

PHK Bank is the only staking authority. All staking rights, records, and distribution rules belong to PHK Bank.

PWB is the execution/interface layer that delivers PHK Bank’s architecture to users and communities.

This is a vertical structure: PHK Bank defines the rules, PWB executes the experience.

Staking Policy Update

NOTICE: PWB no longer accepts new staking.

All staking previously made through PWB is automatically recognized and preserved as contribution to PHK Bank.

If your staking period has ended (or you want to add more), you can re-stake via PHK Bank.

What stays protected
Your staking record, participation history, and distribution weight remain valid under PHK Bank.
What changes
PWB stops receiving new staking. New/renewed staking is done only in PHK Bank.

No speculation. No imaginary growth. No financial fiction.
